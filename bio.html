Iris Warchall
she/her/hers

  • Bachelor of Science, Neuroscience, College of William and Mary, 2009
  • Doctorate of Physical Therapy, University of Southern California, 2013

My paintings are strongly influenced by my professional work as a physical therapist helping people manage and overcome chronic pain and functional loss. I find the process of balancing color, composition, and movement in a piece to be very similar to the analysis of movement, posture, and miscellaneous contextual factors that a person may present with during a physical therapy evaluation.
